Privacy Policy

Last updated August 09, 2025

UrbanFabric ElevenGen is a desktop plug-in and does not operate a public service.

What we collect

  • No account data. We don’t run signup or cloud accounts.
  • No analytics. The plug-in does not send usage analytics.
  • API keys are stored locally in the operating system keychain.
  • Generated audio is cached locally on your machine.

What we don’t collect

  • We do not store your prompts or audio on our servers.
  • We do not re-use or re-train on your content.
