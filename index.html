AUv3 • Logic Pro (macOS on Apple silicon) • iPadOS (planned)

What it does

In-DAW generation

Type a prompt, pick duration, generate. Results load directly into the instrument so you can play, preview, and print.

Stems & variations

Request drums-only, bass-only, or chords, then nudge the vibe with “try again” variations.

Tempo & key aware

Auto-conform to project BPM/key for instant audition without manual editing.

Respectful & safe

Blocks artist impersonation and disallowed content. Keys are stored in the system keychain.

How it works

  1. Insert UrbanFabric ElevenGen on a Software Instrument track.
  2. Enter a prompt & duration and click Generate.
  3. Play to audition; drag resulting audio into the timeline.

ElevenGen uses your own ElevenLabs API key. We never transmit your key to our servers, and we don’t log prompts.
